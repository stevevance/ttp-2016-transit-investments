This page provides information on fixed-guideway transit projects across North America, up to date as of January 2016. It was designed by Yonah Freemark and Steven Vance. Source is open and free, and available on GitHub: Webpage code; Geojson shapefiles; CSV spreadsheets of projects. Use this code to embed a Transit Explorer map on your website. Please report any errors to info@thetransportpolitic.com or using the form on the left by clicking "Find an error?"

Sources:
  • Baseline data on existing U.S. transit systems from the U.S. Bureau of Transportation Statistics.
  • Existing Canadian and Mexican transit lines from Open Street Map.
  • New York City Subway, Metro-North Railroad, and Long Island Rail Road from Baruch College.
Note: "Regions" defined for each project do not denote municipality or even state. They denote the metropolitan area in which the project exists or is planned. Map rendering powered by Scout.
Recent changes: San Juan Tren Urbano, NICTD South Shore Extension, Metra new stations, Morgantown PRT added; Seattle light rail, Denver R line routes altered.
